What this page shows

  • Symbol and name. Public-market ticker and the company it identifies.
  • Weight. The position's share of the current book at the build timestamp.
  • Return since entry. Current market value over weighted-average cost basis. Positive returns shown in green; negative in red. Positions with no recorded basis show a dash.
  • Entry. The month of the earliest surviving lot.
  • Tranche. The internal classification used in the portfolio's tier framework -- Anchor (high-conviction core), T1-T3 (probabilistic scaling), Vault (capital-protection layer), Probe (small thesis test).
  • Thesis node. The specific pattern the position indexes (e.g., space_eo, nuclear_fuel, critical_minerals). Holdings are grouped above by higher-level thesis pillar.

What this page does not show

Share counts. Dollar amounts. Cost basis values in dollars. Cash balances. Active pending orders. These live in the private repository this site is built from; they do not leave it. The boundary is enforced programmatically by a sanitization layer with regression tests against the canonical data.
